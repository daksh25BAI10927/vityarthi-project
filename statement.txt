# Statement: Expense Tracker

## Project Title
Expense Tracker (Console-based, OOP)

## Problem Statement
Users often lose track of daily spending. This local, offline expense tracker helps users record and analyze expenses.

## Scope
- Add and view expenses with dates and notes.
- Sorting and grouping.
- JSON-based persistence.

## Target Users
Students and individuals wanting a simple expense recorder.

## High-level Features
- Add expense (today's date)
- View all expenses
- Sort by amount
- Group by date
